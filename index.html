Who are we? Our Mission

The mission of the ecology club is to provide students with hands-on opportunities to impact the environment outside of the classroom walls and in their everyday lives.

Join Today!

What do we do? Our Vision

The vision of the ecology club is to work in the outdoor lab to sustain and attract native wildlife. We are also focused on using recycled materials to improve the outdoor lab.

The Sponsor Mrs. Bono

Mrs. Bono is the STEM teacher and ecology club sponsor. She has taught at Pleasant Grove since 2007. She enjoys teaching students about the world around them and how they can improve it.

Who can join? Potential Members

Fourth and fifth grade students are able to join the ecology club at the beginning of each school year. An announcement will be made when applications are ready for pick-up.
