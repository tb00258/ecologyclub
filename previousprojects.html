Previous Projects A glimpse in the past

The ecology club has played a crucial role at the school. The club bridges school-wide projects and STEM units to the everyday lives of students.

2014 The pond

Ecology club members and STEM summer campers worked together to establish the school pond. Club members help clean the pond filters and scrub away stubborn algae. The pond is a relexing retreat for students and staff to getaway. It is also a great spot for seeing a sustainable ecosystem in person.

2016 Recycled Planters

Students repurposed two-liter bottles by creating hanging planters. Not only does this save the bottles from the landfill but it makes the outdoor space a little more colorful too.
